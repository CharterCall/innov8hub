About Innov8Hub

Bridging the gap between complex technology and real-world small business operations.

Founder – Benjamin Alibuyog

Strategy • Data • Operations

Benjamin Alibuyog founded Innov8Hub with a simple goal: to make AI and automation practical and accessible for small businesses.

With a background in strategy, data, and business operations, Benjamin has spent years working inside large, complex organisations. He helped teams improve how they operate, make decisions, and deliver better outcomes for customers. Through that experience, he saw a clear gap — the kinds of tools and efficiencies used by large companies were often out of reach for small businesses.

"Innov8Hub was created to bridge that gap. We translate complex technology into simple, real-world solutions."

Benjamin focuses on solutions that save time, reduce manual work, and improve how businesses run day-to-day. His approach is hands-on and outcome-driven — not just implementing tools, but ensuring they genuinely make a difference.

He’s particularly passionate about helping business owners reclaim their time, streamline operations, and build systems that allow them to grow without adding unnecessary complexity.
